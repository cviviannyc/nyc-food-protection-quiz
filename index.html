Why this exists

Built by an operator who needed to pass it too.

I'm Vivian — founder of Lumi Vending, a Queens-based vending and micro market operator. The NYC Food Protection Certificate is required to run hot food and unattended retail in food service settings, so I built this for myself first.

The official course material is comprehensive but reads like a textbook. There was no built-in way to test myself before sitting the exam — so I made one. It now covers all 15 lessons plus the official DOH study guide, and it's free for anyone preparing for the same certification.

How to use it

Most people benefit from flashcards first to build recall on temperatures and pathogens, then quiz mode to simulate exam conditions. Use the topic filters to focus on the lessons you're weakest on. Review your missed answers at the end of each round.

Not affiliated with DOHMH

This is an independent study tool. It is not an official NYC Department of Health product, and using it does not certify anyone. The official course is at nyc.gov/health.
